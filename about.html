Our Story

Veloura Travels was founded in 2024 by a group of travellers who believed the best journeys are those perfectly tailored to the individual. Over the past 2 years, we've helped over 3,000 travellers discover the world's most breathtaking destinations.

From private island escapes in Thailand to luxury desert camps in Dubai, every package we offer is personally vetted, thoughtfully designed, and supported by our team 24/7.
